Welcome to the Website of the Most Famous Rugby Tournament in the World

The 6 Nations Tournament of 2009 runs from Saturday 7th February 2009 to Saturday 21st March 2009.

It is comprised of 15 matches between Ireland, Engand, Scotland, Wales, France and Italy.

Each team achieves 2 points for winning a match, 1 point for drawing a match, and 0 points for losing a match.

The team which collects most points is the Six Nations Champion.

If the Six Nations Champion wins all 5 of their matches they are additionally awarded the notional title of Grand Slam Champions.

Any one of the four home nations (Ireland, England, Scotland, Wales) which wins all matches against the other home nations is awarded the notional title of Triple Crown Champions.
